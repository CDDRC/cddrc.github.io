Overview

The Cardiovascular Development Data Resource Center (CDDRC) is the newest component of the National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute (NHLBI) Bench-to-Bassinet (B2B) consortia, which includes the Pediatric Cardiac Genomics Consortium (PCGC) and the Pediatrics Heart Network (PHN)

Mission

The mission of the CDDRC is to support genetics and genomics research into the causes of congenital heart disease (CHD) by assembling data from B2B investigators together with external datasets from the cardiovascular development community and to provide a searchable data platform and suite of analysis tools.

Headquartered At The University Of Utah

The CDDRC is maintained by a team of developers, data scientists and geneticists located at the University of Utah in the Department of Human Geneitcs and the Utah Center for Genetic Discovery.
