CDDRC Overview

The Cardiovascular Development Data Resource Center (CDDRC) is the newest component of the National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute (NHLBI) Bench-to-Bassinet (B2B) consortia, which includes the Pediatric Cardiac Genomics Consortium (PCGC) and the Pediatrics Heart Network (PHN)

Mission Statement

The mission of the CDDRC is to support genetics and genomics research into the causes of congenital heart disease (CHD) by assembling data from B2B investigators together with external datasets from the cardiovascular development community and to provide a platform and suite of analysis tools

Headquartered At The University Of Utah
